The Sidi Story

Founded in Nairobi, Sidi Scents embodies the essence of African luxury. Each fragrance is meticulously crafted to capture the vibrant spirit and rich heritage of our continent.

Our perfumers blend traditional African botanicals with modern perfumery techniques to create unique, long-lasting scents that tell a story.
